The Wild Circus Show

About Us

Wild Circus was created by acrobats, dancers, film-makers and musicians in 1986. Wild Circus has performed in 28 countries and designs shows for sports events, fun days, festivals, schools and environmental charities. Wild Circus artistic team has over 25 years of experience touring action-packed comedy shows in big tops, street and theatres.
